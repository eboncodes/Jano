## Vision
Jano AI School is an educational application designed for high school students in Bangladesh. The app leverages AI teachers to provide a comprehensive, interactive, and accessible learning experience. Students can study concise versions of their textbooks, receive personalized help, and participate in a vibrant AI-powered school ecosystem.

---

## Brand Identity
- **Name:** Jano
- **Primary Colors:** Light Purple, Yellow, White
- **Font:** Geist

---

## Core Features

### 1. AI Teachers
- Interactive AI-powered teachers for each subject.
- Natural language Q&A and explanations.
- Personalized study recommendations.

### 2. Book Summaries
- Short, easy-to-understand versions of high school textbooks (Bangladesh curriculum).
- Visual aids, diagrams, and key points highlighted.

### 3. Study Help & Tutoring
- On-demand help with homework and concepts.
- Step-by-step problem solving.
- Practice quizzes and instant feedback.

### 4. AI School Ecosystem
- Virtual classrooms and subject rooms.
- Progress tracking and achievements.
- Peer-to-peer Q&A (optional, moderated by AI).
- Daily/weekly challenges and leaderboards.

### 5. User Experience
- Clean, modern UI with brand colors and Geist font.
- Multilingual support (Bangla & English).
- Accessibility features (text-to-speech, dyslexia-friendly mode, etc.).
